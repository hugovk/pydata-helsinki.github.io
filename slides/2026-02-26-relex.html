PyData Helsinki

PyData is a global network of users and developers of data analysis tools

This means Pandas, NumPy, PySpark, etc — and also R, Julia, and other non-Python tools

And statistics, neural networks, databases, visualization, and more

Focus on open source tools and their real-world use
